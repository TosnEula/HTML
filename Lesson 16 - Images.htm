Lesson 16 - Images

background

Setting a background image

The background-image property allows you to place an image behind any HTML element.

Repeating Images and following

background-repeat is used to have the background image be repeated

  • repeat
  • repeat-x
  • repeat-y
  • no-repeat


background-attachment does sets the background image to follow the user as they scroll.

  • fixed
  • scroll

Background positioning

background-position sets the background image's position.

  • left top
  • left center
  • left bottom
  • center top
  • center center
  • center bottom
  • right top
  • right center
  • right bottom

background shorthand

Using background followed by:

  1. background-color
  2. background-image
  3. background-repeat
  4. background-attachment
  5. background-position
